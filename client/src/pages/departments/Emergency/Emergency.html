The Emergency Unit operates 24 hours a day, staffed by highly qualified doctors and nurses, supported by a team of specialists and senior consultants in various fields.
The unit is equipped to handle all emergency cases, accidents, surgeries, neurological disorders, heart diseases, gastrointestinal issues, and intestinal diseases.
The Emergency Unit is backed by intensive care units in all specialties.

The Emergency Unit comprises 12 ready beds to receive patients at any time around the clock.
Our emergency team, responsible for accidents, operates 24/7 and has the ability to handle multiple specialties such as general surgery, neurosurgery, cardiac and thoracic surgeries, orthopedic surgery, facial and jaw surgery.
The unit includes a highly qualified stroke team.

The ambulances of Egypt International Hospital operate 24/7, staffed with highly qualified doctors and nurses, supported by a team of specialists and senior consultants in various fields.

The ambulance is equipped to handle all emergencies, accidents, surgeries, neurological disorders, heart diseases, digestive system issues, and intestinal diseases.

We have three modern ambulances equipped with advanced medical equipment, functioning as mini-intensive care units.

MISR International Hospital is the largest private critical care provider in Giza, Egypt. The ICU is staffed by a globally equipped and multi-specialty team of consultant experts, critical care residents, intensive care nurses, pharmacists, physical therapists, nutrition specialists, nurses, and experts in acute pain management.


Patients in our ICU have 24/7 access to advanced ventilation, continuous hemodynamic monitoring systems, various methods of treatment for renal problems, as well as a modern and efficient radiology department. When necessary, referrals are made to consultants from our group of medical and surgical specialties.
