Fractures and Sprains

For bone-related conditions, we can provide a quick diagnosis through magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) and ultrasound scans at the site. Experts in musculoskeletal devices can offer their opinions and consultations on treatment and care.

MISR International Hospital is capable of treating a variety of bone injuries and wounds. Our specialized team of highly skilled doctors and experienced nurses can assess and diagnose sprains, fractures, and sports injuries.

We have a group of specialized consultants to address more complex muscle injuries.

Sprain injuries are diagnosed through physical examination. In most cases, X-rays are taken of the affected joint to ensure no fractures. If there is a suspected tear in ligaments or soft tissues, magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) is obtained. MRI is usually performed after swelling has subsided and can easily identify ligament injuries. A follow-up appointment with a specialist can be arranged if necessary.

Radiological Diagnosis

Treatment for Fractures and Sprains

* Pain relievers

* Orthopedic realignment (neck, back, shoulder, wrist, elbow, knee, ankle)

* Compression therapy

* Casting and bracing

We can also arrange hospital admission and provide short-term and long-term follow-up through our specialized orthopedic and sports medicine consultants.

Sports Injuries and Exercise

We offer treatment for injuries resulting from sports or exercise, including sprains, knee injuries, and thigh injuries.

Rapid Treatment for Sports Injuries

Our care teams provide a quick examination and treatment for the most common sports injuries. We have rapid access to diagnostic imaging and blood tests, along with a large number of leading specialist doctors in MISR International Hospital. Specialists in bone care or sports and sports medicine.

If your injury requires continuous treatment or monitoring, we can arrange all your ongoing care, whether under a consultant or through supportive services such as physical therapy.

Evaluation of Sports Injuries

You will first see a screening nurse who will assess the severity of your injury before presenting you to a care physician. The doctor will then conduct a comprehensive physical examination and assess whether you need any other diagnostic tests.

If the doctor believes you will need ongoing treatment, they can refer you to one of our leading consultants, specializing in either sports medicine and exercise or orthopedic surgery.

Diagnostic Radiology for Sports Injuries

We have access to leading diagnostic radiology services, with common imaging procedures used in sports injuries:

* X-rays

* Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) and 3D MRI

* Ultrasound

* Computed Tomography (CT) scans

Treatment for Sports Injuries

* Pain relief

* Rapid recovery techniques

* Referral to a specialist, possibly surgery if needed

* Referral to physical therapy

We can arrange a specialized referral for short or long-term follow-up and hospital admission for ongoing treatment if necessary.

The Cardiac and Vascular Surgery Department at MISR International Hospital provides surgical solutions for cases related to the heart, lungs, and blood vessels.

Comprehensive Cardiac and Vascular Surgery (CTVS) is offered at a dedicated Heart Center, addressing diseases affecting organs within the chest (heart, lungs, and major blood vessels).

The center aims to provide excellent surgical care with a focus on reducing pain, cost, and inconvenience for patients, ultimately restoring their health and well-being.

A wide range of complex heart problems is managed through advanced surgical care with the assistance of fully-equipped, state-of-the-art Cardiac Wings. Operation rooms and other services are arranged in a patient-friendly manner for added comfort. The advanced CTVS ICU unit is well-equipped to provide 24-hour pre and post-surgery care for critically ill patients. The surgical team at MISR International Hospital consists of highly qualified surgeons to ensure optimal care during and after surgical treatments. IABP (Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump) and ECMO (Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation) are utilized to support the lives of critically ill patients experiencing respiratory and/or heart failure.

The procedures require skilled professionals and doctors with the latest technologies, which MISR International Hospital ensures for its patients.
