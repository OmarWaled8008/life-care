Abdominal and Stomach Disorders


We provide treatment for digestive system symptoms, including abdominal pain, bloating, or loss of appetite.

MISR International Hospital Urgent Care Centers offer treatment for a wide range of stomach and intestinal issues. We are capable of providing care for liver, pancreas, or bile duct (liver) issues, which may manifest with symptoms of stomach or abdominal pain.

With access to a large number of specialists, once our initial investigations and treatments are completed, we can arrange the next most suitable step—whether it be reassurance, follow-up with a general practitioner, specialized consultation, or urgent admission to the network of MISR International Hospital for further imaging, treatment, or surgery if necessary.

Common Stomach and Intestinal Symptoms Experienced by Our Patients Include:

*Abdominal pain

*Bloating, vomiting, and nausea

*Diarrhea and constipation

*Fever

*Jaundice

*Loss of appetite

*Rectal bleeding

How Can We Support Abdominal and Intestinal Disorders?

We understand that symptoms related to stomach and intestinal issues can be distressing; however, it's important to consult a doctor promptly.

Our urgent care physicians can conduct an initial assessment and diagnosis, offering their opinion on whether the patient needs treatment from a specialist. If necessary, we can refer you to top consultants at MISR International Hospital, including gastroenterologists, liver disease specialists, general surgeons, and specialized colorectal surgeons.

Diagnostic Imaging and Procedures for Abdominal and Stomach Disorders

We have access to leading imaging tests and investigative procedures for stomach, liver, pancreas, and intestinal conditions.

Diagnostic and Investigative Procedures You May Be Referred to Include::

*Computed Tomography (CT) scans

*X-rays

*Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)

*Fiber optic endoscopy

*Colonoscopy

*Gastroscopy

Urgent Care Treatment for Abdominal and Intestinal Issues May Include::

*Full assessment and examination

*Pain relief

*Antibiotics (intravenous or oral)

For more information about hospital departments, please contact us on the hotline: 16010

Respiratory System Disorders

At Egypt International Hospital, we address respiratory and chest complaints, including asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), breathing pain or difficulty, chest infections, shortness of breath, and non-cardiac chest pains.

Respiratory System Issues

Our emergency and critical care teams at Egypt International Hospital provide immediate examination, investigation, and treatment for most respiratory or chest complaints.

In our emergency care section, we can conduct blood tests, X-rays, and specialized CT scans within the hospital. We also have quick access to a network of specialized consultants in respiratory diseases or cardiac issues, whom we can refer you to for same-day or within a few days appointments, depending on the urgency of the problem.

If the issue requires hospital admission, we can arrange your entry into Egypt International Hospital as quickly as possible.

Please note that in some emergency cases, specialized cardiac treatment may not be available. If you experience severe chest pain or symptoms of a heart attack, please call 16010.

Common Respiratory/Chest Complaints We Typically Address:


* Asthma and Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

* Breathing pain/difficulty

* Chest infections

* Coughing

* Shortness of breath

* Non-cardiac chest pain

* Palpitations


Assessment for Chest and Respiratory Diseases


* Medical history and thorough examination

* Electrocardiogram (ECG)

Blood Tests for Chest and Respiratory Diseases

* Routine and specialized blood tests are available


Diagnostic Imaging for Chest and Respiratory Diseases

* Chest X-rays

* Computed Tomography (CT) scans

* Ultrasound

Treatment for Chest and Respiratory Diseases

* Pain relievers

* Specialized anticoagulant medication

* Administration of oxygen, inhalers, and/or nebulizers as needed

* Antibiotics


We can arrange a specialized referral for short or long-term follow-up and hospital admission for ongoing treatment if necessary.

For more information about hospital departments, please contact us on the hotline: 16010

The Rheumatology Department at Egypt International Hospital has a fully qualified team of doctors offering sophisticated treatment regimens to provide comfort for rheumatism patients and help them live a pain-free life.

Patients experiencing joint stiffness or any type of tissue and bone disorders, including muscle and skeletal pain, can visit a rheumatologist to exclude joint inflammation or receive timely treatment. Egypt International Hospital is equipped with commonly used imaging tools such as ultrasound, polarized light microscopy, C-Arm, and capillaroscopy for accurate diagnosis.

In addition to routine immunological tests conducted by rheumatologists to achieve optimal patient benefit, various pain relief techniques are also offered. These include stellate ganglion block for patients with complex regional pain syndrome, joint injections in the face, transforaminal injections, dorsal root ganglion block, and blockage of digestive disorders and sacrococcygeal dry needling.

Our hospital has well-qualified physiotherapists who can provide advice to patients on the recommended treatment regimen.

The Rheumatology Department specializes in diagnosing and providing the latest advanced care and treatment for all rheumatic problems and associated complications according to international guidelines.
